Review

Tristen J Hum February 22nd 2023

Harry Potter is a staple in my household I grew up reading the books. I would rate this movie a 10/10 overall movie. From the deep ties to family to the way the directors kept key aspects of the book. Seeing my favorite book characters brough to life in such a way brings so much joy to me. I think the VFX artists also did an amazing job with the magical beasts and blending them seamlessly into the movie. I also loved the casting choices because you could see the chemistry the cast has with each other

Trivia

  • Richard Harris who plays Albus Dumbledore initially had trouble remembering his lines which a young Daniel Radcliffe solved by asking for help running his own lines.
  • During the scenes in which the characters were doing school work the child actors would actually be doing their schoolwork to not only multitask but also make it more realistic
  • The Hogwart's motto "draco dormiens nunquam titillandus" translates to "Never tickle a sleeping dragon"
